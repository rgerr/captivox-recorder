# Privacy

Captivox Recorder runs entirely on your machine. The application captures microphone and system audio locally and writes recordings and transcripts to a session folder on your computer. No audio, transcripts, or usage data are sent to external servers.

## Data Storage

Recorded audio and generated transcripts remain in the session folder under your user profile. You may delete or archive session folders at any time.

## Telemetry

Captivox Recorder does not collect or transmit telemetry. If you choose to support development via donation, we will receive your PayPal e‑mail as part of the transaction; this information is handled by PayPal and not stored by us.

## Contact

If you have questions about privacy, please open an issue or contact us at the security contact address in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
